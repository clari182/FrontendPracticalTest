Login

Hey there! This popup gives you the posibility to login and save it in a cookie. You just need to click on the button.

You can reload the page and still be logged in (the cookie name is "username"). If you click on Logout, the cookie will be destroyed and the cookie monster will starve.

Warning! Since the site isn't published yet you must try this function on IE or Firefox. Chrome doesn't allow cookie saving from local files. You can also start chrome with the --enable-file-cookies flag and it will work.
